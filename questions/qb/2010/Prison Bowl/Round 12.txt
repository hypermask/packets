These substances exhibit Shockley-Read-Hall and Auger [oh-ZHAY] recombination. One type has a temperature dependence governed by Fermi-Dirac statistics. The intrinsic type has a small but non-zero band gap between the valence and conduction bands. Charge carriers in these materials include both electrons and holes.
