By mass, most of the atmosphere is contained within this layer, as it is the densest. answer: {prime} numbers [or {primes}]

The fundamental theorem of arithmetic states that every positive integer can be uniquely represented as a product of these numbers. Special types of these numbers are named after Fermat (“fur-MAHT”) and Mersenne (“mur-SEN”). To find these numbers, one may use the Sieve of Eratosthenes (air-uh-TOSS- then-eez”), in which one crosses off all multiples of two, then all multiples of three, and so on. For 10 points, give these numbers whose only factors are one and themselves.
