answer: {adrenaline} [or {epinephrine}; do not accept {noradrenaline} or {norepinephrine}]

The release of this catecholamine cannot be controlled in pheochromocytoma, and both it and an unmethylated precursor are transported by VMAT1. While it triggers vasoconstriction in smooth muscle, it dilates blood vessels in skeletal muscle.
