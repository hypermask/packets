Section 4 of this law allows those injured in its violation to seek treble damages, which resulted in the case Kansas v. Utilicorp United Inc., and another section notes that “The labor of a human being is not a commodity or article of commerce.”  It was amended by the Celler-Kefauver Act, and it notably does not regulate collective bargaining or picketing, and thus does not impact union activities like its predecessor, but it does prohibit mergers and acquisitions when they may be used to substantially lessen competition. answer: {Rorschach Inkblot} Test [prompt on {Inkblot Test}]

A lesser-known method for scoring this test is known as Bohm's system.  The more commonly used method, the Exner system, takes into account factors such as location, form, color, movement and frequency.  An attempt to improve it is known as the Holtzman test, which provides more objective scoring criteria and increases the number of test items from ten to forty-five.  The original ten test items consist of three multicolored figures, two black and red figure, and five black figures on white paper, all of which are open to interpretation.  For 10 points, name this psychological test that asks participants to identify objects in ambiguous pictures that resemble drops of ink.
